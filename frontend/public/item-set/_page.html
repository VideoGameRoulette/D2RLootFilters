Valuable Unique and Set Items

by DarkHumility
Last Updated: March 6, 2024

There are many amazing Unique and Set items that you may come across in your travels throughout Sanctuary, RNG willing of course. A sizeable portion of these are incredibly valuable, either due to their rarity, or their use in many of the best builds in the game. Generally, targeting Elites and Bosses with high Magic Find in Low Player Count (/Players1-3) games is the most efficient way to acquire Sets and Uniques. This listing helps you to quickly identify which are worth to keep for Trading, use on your Character's build, or if they are "Trash" tier useless.

Trade Value

All D2R Unique and Set items have been placed into different value categories. Items listed with a High, Medium (Med), or Low value are typically easy to trade (tradable) and/or are best in slot options for a build. Items listed with a value of None can be useful for builds, but have little to no trade value or are difficult to trade. Items listed as "TRASH" are rarely if ever used for anything. Keep in mind that the relative value of items changes over the course of a Ladder season. Some of them may have extremely high value early on, but drop off later (e.g. Tal Rasha's Guardianship). This relative value listing is most accurate at the beginning of a Ladder reset.
